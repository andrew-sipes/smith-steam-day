All About Macaws

Macaws are vibrant and majestic parrots known for their colorful plumage and sociable nature. These large birds are native to Central and South America, where they inhabit forests and woodlands. Macaws are highly intelligent, often forming strong bonds with their human caretakers. They are also renowned for their loud vocalizations, which serve both as communication and as a way to establish their territory. Macaws have impressive lifespans and can live up to 60 years in captivity, making them popular pets among bird enthusiasts.
