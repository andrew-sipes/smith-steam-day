Hello, Smith Middle School!

My name is Andrew Sipes.

I will be conducting web design and development sessions on April 9th for STEAM Day.

My aim is to showcase web design and development as a means of self-expression and to demonstrate that starting is simpler than you imagine.

For the class activity, students will work together to build their own web page. They will pick from a preset list of colors, layouts, and assets. The web pages will not contain any personally identifiable information.
